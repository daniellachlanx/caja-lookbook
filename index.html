Misuse · don'ts
✦ Do
  • Keep navy dominant, red a sparing accent, gold for hairlines only.
  • Use the reversed mark on dark or photographic backgrounds.
  • Give every lockup its full one-star-height of clear space.
  • Drop to the simpler submark when space is tight.
✕ Don't
  • Remove the red offset from the wordmark, or add offset where it isn't specified.
  • Stretch, condense, rotate, or add glow / bevel / drop-shadow to any mark.
  • Recolour outside the four brand colours, or fill type in gold.
  • Swap the star for the 柔道 kanji in the primary lockup.
  • Place the cream-ground mark on a busy photo without the knockout.

Misuse · don'ts
✦ Do
  • Keep it black & white; spend the single red accent sparingly.
  • Let white space and strong horizontal rules carry the layout.
