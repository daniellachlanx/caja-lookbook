Realistic count: a small club needs roughly 4–6 distinct marks (primary + a horizontal & stacked lockup, wordmark, symbol, submark, optional crest), each in 3 colour variants — not dozens. If a mark has no home on a real surface (gi patch, header, avatar, flyer, certificate, merch), you don't need it.

Now in the lookbook: the usage-rules layer (clear-space, colour spec, do/don’ts) is built for all three directions, and Old North has its full mark family & favicons — see index.html. Assets live in per-direction slot folders (see ASSETS.md).

Sequence: pick one primary symbol and one primary lockup first — everything else (submark, crest, variants, embroidery version) derives from those two. Don't build alternates before the primary is locked.
